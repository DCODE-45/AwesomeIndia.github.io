About India

India is one of the oldest civilization in the world with a kaleidoscopic variety and rich culture heritage.It has achieved all around socio-economic progress during the last seven decades.India has become self sufficient in all areas like agriculture ,tourism ,military ,industry ,IT and now one of the top reputed countries in the world and one of the few nations to have gone into outer space to conquer the nature for the benefit of the people.It covers an area of 32,87,263 sq.km extending from the snow-covered Himalayas in the north,it stretches southwards and at the Tropic of Cancer,tapers off into the Indian Ocean between Bay of Bengal on the east and the Arabian sea on the west.

Jammu & Kashmir

Jammu and Kashmir, state of India, located in the northern part of the Indian subcontinent in the vicinity of the Karakoram and westernmost Himalayan mountain ranges.

Punjab

The Land of Five Rivers, is situated in the North Western region of India.Chandigarh and Amritsar, the great centre of Sikh pilgrimage are the two main tourist’s attractions.

Kerala

Kerala, a very prominent state in India, known as the “God’s Own Country” is a coastal state. Kerala is a tourist destination attracting people by its natural beauty.
