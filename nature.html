Nature

From hauntingly beautiful snowy escapades to lush forests and hills of rolling green, from the endless golden beaches to the sapphire and emerald lakes embedded in the landscape, the natural beauty of the Indian subcontinent is unparalleled. The unspoilt and unexplored terrains of the country leave one in awe of their breathtaking splendour. Moreover, the differences and contrasts of one region from another accentuate the appeal of these landscapes - be it the frigid cold deserts of Leh that call to the soul-searcher in you or the sun-drenched mystique desert dunes of Rajasthan that take you back to the times of royals. Starting from the snow-capped Himalayas in the north, traversing the deserts of Rajasthan, crossing the lush forests of Madhya Pradesh and then sailing in the expansive backwaters of Kerala, and finally diving into the pristine waters of the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, the journey is full of adventurous highs and serene lows.

Popular Nature Destinations In India

Dudhsagar Waterfall

Dudhsagar waterfall is also known as sea of Milk is a four-tiered waterfall located in Goa and surrounded by western ghats and home to rich bio diversity.

Sambhar Salt Lake

Sambhar Salt Lake of Rajasthan is India's largest inland salt lake. It covers area of 5.1km and is the source of most of Rajasthan's salt production.

Ooty

Ooty is a quaint hill town known as 'Switzerland of India'. It is lined with traditional and colonial type buildings which makes it the perfect fusion of the new and the old.

Manali

The magnetic town of Manali beckons to the soul of adventures, nature lovers as well as solace-seekers. It is specially known for its FLoral meadows and snow-capped mountains.

Coorg

Coorg is a tiny hill station famously known as the 'Scotland of India'.This place is irresistible with its lush coffee plantations,stunning landscapes and cascading waterfalls.

Valley of Flowers

Valley of Flowers is a national park located in Himalays of Uttarakhand known for its meadows of endemic alpine and rare and endangered animals.

Snow Valley

Snow Valley is the most beautiful place in India, located in Kashmir. Kashir also known as Paradise on Earth, is known for its captivating beauty and surrounding highlands.

White Desert

The white desert is a salt marshy land in Gujarat. It is specially known for its 'The Rann Utsav' the festival celebrated every year between December to February.

Lonar Crater

Lonar lake is one of the must see natural wonders of India, located in Maharashtra. This lake is worlds's oldest meteoric crater.

Sundarban

Sundarban swamp forest is the largest single block of mangrove on the Ganges Delta and is the largest reserves forest for the Bengal tigers in India.
